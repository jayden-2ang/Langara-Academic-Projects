Some Great Restaurants in Vancouver:

Nanaimo Sushi
Nanaimo Sushi is a famously-known place within Vancouver that serves a great assortment of sushi. Their menu consists of Bento-boxes, sushi platters, and Pork or Chicken Katsu on rice.
Yu Xiang Yuan Restaurant
Yu Xiang Yuan is a great restaurant known for their handmade and unique noodles that they make right in front of you! They have an assortment of noodles in soups and dumplings which are very tasty.
Yolks
Yolks is a great breakfast place to visit if you're in the mood for some tasty egg benedicts, omelettes, or anything to do with eggs!
Western Lake
Western Lake is a famous dim sum place known for having delicious and tasty dishes. Visit Western Lake if you're ever craving some dim sum and it will not dissapoint.
Steveston Pizza
A great pizzaria that serves a great assortment of pizzas.

Some Great Dessert places:
