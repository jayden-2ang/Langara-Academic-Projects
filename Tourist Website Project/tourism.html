Tourism/Attractions:

Grouse

Grouse Mountain is a place in BC famous for its winter activities.

Whistler

Whistler is a mountain also famous for many winter activities.

Granville Island

Granville Island is a very popular tourist attraction in Vancouver. Because it is very close to the ocean, there are all kinds of different seafood and unique gift shops.

Fisherman's Wharf in Steveston

This is a picture of Fishermen's Wharf, in Steveston. It is one of our tourist attractions as there are plenty of seafood and different cuisines around there.

Stanley Park

Stanley park is probably the most well-known park in Vancouver. There are many activities to do there like going on a picnic, having a BBQ, or even just cycling around the park.

Central Park

Central Park is a park located in Burnaby and is known for its vast area and its beautiful nature.
